<i>GeoGas</i> allows users to locate the cheapest gas stations in their area, facilitating fuel savings through a geolocation system and real-time price comparison.

<b>Key Features:</b>

* Automatic Geolocation: Detects your location and shows the nearest gas stations
* Price Comparison: View up-to-date prices for gasoline, diesel, and other fuels
* Advanced Search: Filter by fuel type, distance, and hours
* Favorites System: Save your favorite gas stations for quick access
